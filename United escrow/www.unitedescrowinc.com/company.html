ABOUT US

 

 

United Escrow, Inc. is a full-service escrow company.  The company is independently owned and licensed by the Department of Business Oversight, (License 9631752). We are members of Los Angeles Escrow Association, California Escrow Association and the Escrow Institute of California, an exclusive achievement held by a selected few companies in this industry.

Our Clients' funds are kept safely in Escrow Trust Account and bonded by the Escrow Fidelity Corporation.

United Escrow Company’s mission is to guarantee our clients the highest level of professional service.  We function as a neutral party for all types of escrow transactions.  In each branch, our officers possess more than 20 years of escrow experience and top producing officers are highly motivated, flexible and knowledgeable professionals who consistently strive to exceed all of our client expectations.  Our associates, who are the primary assets to our company are bilingual proficient in English, Korean and Spanish to further meet our client’s need. 

United Escrow Company is part of a comprehensive real estate industry team, consisting of Realtor, Lender, Builder, Title Officer, Escrow Officer, Transaction Coordinator and Client.  As Escrow Holder and neutral party, we understand the importance of public relations and provide a seamless transaction with excellent communications and services you can depend on.

Our objective is your satisfaction.
