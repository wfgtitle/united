SERVICES

 

United Escrow Company handles a wide variety of real estate transaction and we keep our fees competitive within the local market.  Conveniently located throughout Southern California, we provide inter-branch escrow processing, courier and notarial services to minimize our client's traveling time to accommodate for the successful completion of a transaction to meet our client's every need.

We are committed to offering our clients the most comprehensive escrow services available including:

Expertise

Real Estate Escrow

  • Commercial
  • Residential (Units/SFR/Condo)
  • Industrial
  • Vacant Land/Subdivision
  • Short Sales/Foreclosure
  • New Tract Home

Refinance Escrow

  • Conventional Loans
  • FHA-VA Loans
  • Assumptions/Subject to/AITD
  • 2nd and 3rd Trust Deed Loans
  • Loan escrow

Exchange Escrow

  • IRS Section 1031 Tax Deferred Exchanges

Business (Bulk) Escrow

  • Franchise Business Transaction
  • ABC License transfer
  • Business opportunity/Bulk sales: Restaurant, Market, Car Wash, Cleaners, Gas Station, etc.

Sell-By-Owner Escrow

United Escrow Co. is experienced in the handling of For Sale-By-Owner transaction in a smooth and efficient manner.  We will guide you through the escrow process and supply you with the necessary documentations and disclosures to complete your transaction.
